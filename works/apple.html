Apple

Book 1 of 1

By Jaewon Lee

Releasing October 15, 2025

“Unless a kernel of wheat falls to the ground and dies, it remains only a single seed. But if it dies, it produces many seeds.”
—John 12:24 (NIV)

Every parent wants a children's storybook to be appealing, fun, and full of life lessons. There is no other book that meets the requirement better than Apple.

The story follows Apple, who waits quietly in a tree and wonders when his time will come. With warmth and gentle humor in the book, young readers are drawn into a colorful adventure with Apple that sparks curiosity and encourages imagination.

Parents will appreciate positive message woven into the tale — a reminder that even the tiniest beginnings can grow into a wonderful life.

Ideal for bedtime or classroom reading, Apple is more than a story. It is an invitation for children to giggle, dream, and imagine together — even when their ideas are a little bit silly.
