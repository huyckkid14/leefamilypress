K-Story Songs

The stories in our book (K-Story: History, Legends & Songs of Korea) were never meant to rest on the page alone—they were made to be heard. Here, Korea’s oldest songs find new life through modern voices and melodies that echo across time. Though their original tunes have vanished through centuries, they rise again here—revived so that the music of Korea’s ancestors may be heard anew, like timeless echoes of history itself.
